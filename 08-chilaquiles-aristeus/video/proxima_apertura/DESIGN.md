# Design System: Aristeus - Próxima Apertura

## Colors
- **Background Main:** `#FAF6F0` (Cream/Beige)
- **Primary Accent:** `#C9A96E` (Gold/Metallic)
- **Primary Text:** `#0F172A` (Navy/Slate)
- **Secondary Text:** `#64748B` (Muted Slate)
- **Alert/Hot:** `#E74C3C` (Salsa Red)

## Typography
- **Display:** "DM Serif Display", serif (Elegant, high-contrast)
- **Sans:** "Outfit", sans-serif (Modern, clean, geometric)

## Components
- **Card:** White background, subtle shadow, 1px gold border.
- **Badge:** Gold background, Navy text, 12px border radius.
- **Overlay:** Semi-transparent Navy (`rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.8)`).

## Motion Signatures
- **Entrance:** Power3.out (Smooth deceleration).
- **Transitions:** Elegant crossfades and wipes.
- **Micro-animations:** Subtle scale-up on food assets.

## Brand Voice
- **Vibe:** Ultra-premium, anticipation-driven, artisanal.
- **Focus:** The "Ruta 57" experience and the artisanal quality of the salsas.
