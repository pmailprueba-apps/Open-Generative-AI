# Ruta 57 Heritage Design System

## Concept
A premium, artisanal restaurant brand inspired by the legendary **Ruta 57**. It blends the rustic soul of a road trip with gourmet culinary excellence.

## Colors
- **Chile Red**: `#c0392b` (Primary) - Energy, spice, and passion.
- **Deep Navy**: `#1b2a4a` (Secondary) - Trust, tradition, and nighttime road vibes.
- **Harvest Gold**: `#c9a96e` (Accent) - Premium quality, craftsmanship, and sunlit roads.
- **Artisan Cream**: `#faf6f0` (Background) - Clean, artisanal, and easy on the eyes.

## Typography
- **Heading**: `DM Serif Display` - Elegant, bold, and classic.
- **Body**: `Outfit` - Modern, clean, and highly legible.

## Visual Elements
- **Corner Roundness**: `16px` for a soft, premium feel.
- **Shadows**: Subtle but deep shadows for cards to create a "glass" layered effect.
- **Dividers**: Use gold icons (✦) to separate sections.
