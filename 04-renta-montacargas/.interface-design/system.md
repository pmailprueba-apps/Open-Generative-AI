# Design System: Heavy Duty Industrial

## Direction & Feel
**Premium Industrial / Heavy Duty**. A high-contrast system using Safety Yellow and Deep Carbon to evoke reliability, power, and professionalism in the B2B logistics sector.

## Domain Concepts
- **Tonnage**: Capacidad de carga.
- **Warehouse Flow**: Eficiencia logística.
- **Heavy Duty**: Uso rudo e industrial.
- **Express Quote**: Inmediatez en la respuesta.

## Color Palette
- **Yellow Security** (`#FFB800`): Primary accent for action and visibility.
- **Carbon Black** (`#0A0A0A` / `#121212`): Background and structural depth.
- **Steel Gray** (`#333333`): Borders and secondary elements.

## Depth Strategy
**Subtle Layering / Glassmorphism**. Use of `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.03)` for surfaces and `rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.1)` for borders to create a technical, modern feel without heavy shadows.

## Spacing & Typography
- **Base Unit**: 8px.
- **Typography**: 
  - **Montserrat**: For headings (Heavy weights 800-900).
  - **Inter**: For UI and body text (Precision and readability).

## Key Patterns
- **The Lift Signature**: Slanted logo icons and vertical stacking of information badges.
- **Express Multi-step**: A integrated tool feel for the quotation process rather than a simple form.
